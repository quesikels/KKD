About Me

The Strategist
Behind the Growth

I am a Digital Communications & B2B Growth Strategist with 5+ years of hands-on experience helping businesses across Ghana, South Africa, and internationally grow their digital presence and win better clients.

My work sits at the intersection of strategic communications, B2B marketing, and digital brand development. I have managed 10+ client portfolios simultaneously, led digital activations for West Africa's top communications firm, and published over 100 articles as a journalist and editor in the agricultural media space.

I believe every business problem is, at its core, a communication problem — and that African businesses deserve world-class digital strategy built for their context, not borrowed from markets that look nothing like ours.
